Hi, I'm Rachel!
Originally from Bloomington, Indiana, I moved to New York City a few years ago to attend Columbia University.
Since then, I have fallen in love with the city, especially watching how so many different people live such complex lives, with their own sets of needs, problems, and solutions in a city that is constantly moving and changing.
I use this to inspire my work as a designer, overwhelmed yet excited by the vast world of users, problems, and how to best improve their lives. I focus a lot on how to best streamline the user journey by bringing together the many different people and resources we have in the modern world.
I have extensive experience in UI/UX design - social media, news, e-commerce, productivity, etc. I have particularly found fulfillment in designing visually appealing interfaces, creating detailed and interactive prototypes, and experimenting with how new technologies like GPT-4 can streamline user experiences.
As for teamwork, I am extremely familiar with cross-functional collaboration and an Agile development environment. Furthermore, as a computer science major, I have a deep understanding of front and back end development - I designed and coded this website myself!
Outside of work, I love to boulder. I currently work at Manhattan Plaza Health Club Climbing Gym - let me know if you ever want a day pass :)
