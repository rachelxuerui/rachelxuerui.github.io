Overview

I joined the Product Design team for a summer and helped them in the ideation process for the upcoming Spectator mobile app. This included storyboarding, using red routes to draft an MVP, and contributing to low, medium, and high fidelity prototypes. One of my primary responsibilities was designing a painted door test for the website (mobile) to see whether users would be interested in a mobile app.


Painted door test

On the Product Design team, I worked with Elaine Su and Elise Yang on designing a painted door test. This painted door test would appear in the form of a popup that would prompt users to either open the Spectator app or decline. The purpose of this painted door test was to gauge user interest in a Spectator mobile app.
