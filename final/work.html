act iv - work
i work at a climbing gym and this act documents my typical experience working a shift.
i scan people in and monitor transactions at the front desk, where i hear many clicks, beeps, etc.
sometimes, holds on the climbing walls become loose and must be drilled in.
once it gets emptier, i start climbing, a process i have attempted to depict with ascending scales, which also occasionally requires the brushing of holds.
this shift in particular was worked with my boyfriend, whom i love dearly, hence the cute whimsical melody of this act. <3
i hope you had a nice day <3
