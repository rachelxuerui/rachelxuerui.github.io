Pivoting on an existing idea: personalized stretches for your specific needs
Project overview
This project was completed for the course Innovation and Design Lab at Columbia University.

Our assignment was to choose an app idea proposed by another group, and pivot on the idea.

We decided to pivot on the technology, and use generative AI to generate personalized stretches.

Original app idea

Flexi
"Stretch anyday, anytime, anywhere. No excuses."
Idea and designs by Anuva Banwasi, Rishabh Ganesh, Kentrie Tran, Alina Xu, Bella Chen, Jane Lim
Our main concerns
  1. How does this differ from existing apps?
  2. Are users really motivated to upload videos of their stretches?
  3. Why would people use this app?
Problem
Active individuals often face challenges in creating personalized and effective stretching routines tailored to their specific needs and activities.
Solution
Flexi uses generative AI to create personalized stretching routines based on your inputs.

Integrated with popular health apps, it adapts to your needs, providing smart notifications for morning stretches, pre- and post-workout routines, and real-time adjustments.

Achieve flexibility, prevent injuries, and optimize your fitness journey with Flexi.
