act i - wake
this piece is meant to simulate my typical morning experience.

the melody in this act represents my mind, and the frequency at which is moves throughout the morning.

the kick/bass here represents my heartbeat and alertness, changing throughout the morning appropriately.

i have used samples from my alarm clock, my medication bottle, the water i use to brew coffee, and the spoon i use to stir honey into my morning coffee.

the visuals are clips taken from this morning routine, and the playback speed, blurriness, opacity, and color is adjusted by the FFT from my live coding audio.

i chose these particular factors to simulate a dreamy state of being.

good morning!
