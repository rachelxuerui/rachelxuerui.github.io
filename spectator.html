Overview

I initially joined Spectator Spring 2022 on the Engineering team, working on a trainee project with Katie Chen.

The following summer, as the Engineering team went into hibernation, I joined the Product Design team and helped them in the ideation process for the upcoming Spectator mobile app. This included storyboarding, using red routes to draft an MVP, and contributing to low, medium, and high fidelity mockups. One of my primary responsibilities was designing a painted door test for the website (mobile) to see whether users would be interested in a mobile app.

I rejoined the Engineering team this fall, where I put this painted door test into code. We are about to begin creating the Spectator app in React Native.


Trainee project

The trainee project was a project in React assigned to all new members of Spectator Engineering as an opportunity to learn HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I worked with Katie Chen on this project, and our code can be found here.


Painted door test

On the Product Design team, I worked with Elaine Su and Elise Yang on designing a painted door test. This painted door test would appear in the form of a popup that would prompt users to either open the Spectator app or decline. The purpose of this painted door test was to gauge user interest in a Spectator mobile app.
