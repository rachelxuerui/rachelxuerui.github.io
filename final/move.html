act iii - move
the commute from morningside heights to the lower east side is a familiar one for me, since i go to columbia university and my boyfriend lives by the two bridges.

this act depicts this commute, where i take the 1 to the F, transferring out of the station at 14th street.

i find this commute to be a bit of an adventure every time, challenging myself to see how quickly i can get through the approximately 40-minute trip.

in this act, you will hear honking from 14th street, the sound of the train arriving, sounds simulating my footsteps, as well as an adventurous undertone.

as for the visuals, i adjusted the saturation, playback speed, and motion of the video to simulate my movement through the city.
