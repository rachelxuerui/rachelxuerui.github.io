    Townhouse in Tribeca II
Can't say much about this one yet. Only that it is a vertical expansion of a townhouse in a historic district. If the community board and LPC commissioners will allow it, the addition will have a set of vanishing sliding glass facades that opens the interior entirely to the front and rear roof decks. It will have a pretty neat detail between new and old, and a lyrical Corbusian enclosure to conceal the mechanical units above, made of perforated stainless steel.

As of , we are preparing to present this to the community board in the upcoming meeting.
