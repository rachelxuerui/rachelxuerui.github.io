The Bulletin - Organizer Portal
Helping organizers manage their events and applications on The Bulletin
Project overview
The Bulletin is a web and mobile app designed to serve as a hub for all of Columbia’s events, applications, and organizations.

This project is a redesign of the Organizer Portal - a place where organizers can manage their events and applications.
